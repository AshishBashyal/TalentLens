Platform vision

From raw job posts to business-grade insight.

TalentLens is designed as more than a dashboard. It is a staged platform for data collection, cleaning, analytics, NLP, machine learning, and market intelligence reporting.

01

Collect

Gather job postings from multiple public and permitted sources.

02

Structure

Normalize job titles, companies, locations, salaries, and descriptions.

03

Extract

Use NLP to identify skills, technologies, certifications, and experience.

04

Analyze

Surface hiring trends, salary movement, skill demand, and career signals.

Development roadmap

Incremental by design.

TalentLens starts with structure and documentation, then grows into data pipelines, analytics, NLP, machine learning, and advanced AI features.

  1. Phase 1Foundation, documentation, architecture, landing page.
  2. Phase 2Data collection, cleaning, storage.
  3. Phase 3Analytics and reporting datasets.
  4. Phase 4NLP extraction for skills and technologies.
  5. Phase 5Machine learning and predictive intelligence.
  6. Phase 6Advanced AI recommendations and market reports.

TalentLens is currently in Phase 1: foundation and product architecture.
